About The Kickoo Tribe:

Strengths: Compassionate, artistic, intuitive, gentle, outgoing, easy access to custom houses

Weaknesses: Overly trusting, melancholy, desire to escape reality, no more whales

Like the ships that carry them across the open ocean, Kickoo know how to go with the flow. Kickoo are more intuitive and sensitive than other tribes. They are often happiest when they are all by themselves, either under a vast starry sky or tucked into their comfort zone. They are generous, compassionate and open to new experiences. They are extremely faithful and caring. They are also more tolerant to differences between tribes, however Kickoo can be very sensitive to criticism when directed at them. Kickoo can even become emotionally closed off if they've been hurt too often.

Because they care about other people and the natural world, they'll do whatever they can to make sure the people around them are happy. This willingness to help others can also be a weakness.
